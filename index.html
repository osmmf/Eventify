Frequently asked questions
We have put together some commonly asked questions
How do I create an event?

To create an event, simply click the “Create Event” button on our homepage. Fill in the necessary details such as event name, date, time, location, and description.

Can I edit my event details after creating it?

Yes! You can contact us via email and provide us with all the details you need to edit. Our staff will then contact you to confirm all your updates and assure you that everything will be okay.

What payment methods are accepted for ticket purchases?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other popular payment methods. You’ll see the available options during the checkout process.

What happens if I need to cancel my event?

If you need to cancel, you have to tell us and provide a reason so we can understand. In case we have prepared something before your cancellation, you will be responsible for all the charges.

How can I invite guests to my event?

After the confirmation, you can send us all the contacts that you want to invite, and we will be in charge of inviting them.

Can I customize the event registration form?

Absolutely! When creating an event, you can add custom fields to the registration form, such as dietary preferences or T-shirt sizes.

How do I promote my event to a wider audience?

If you want our help to promote your event, we can assist you, but the charge will be higher. We guarantee that you will see the results you desire and even better.

How do I contact customer support?

Visit our “Contact Us” page or email support@oneclickevent.com. We’re here to assist you!
